# Model 01 — Korea Life System Packs

## Offer
Digital templates for expats/parents in Korea:
- KRW family budget planner
- Family weekly schedule planner
- Korean admin tracker (visa, bills, insurance, school docs)

## Product tiers
- Starter Pack: ₩9,900
- Full Pack: ₩19,900

## Target customer
- English-speaking expats in Korea
- Busy parents (especially dual-language households)

## 14-day launch (execution)
1. Build 3 MVP templates
2. Publish simple sales page
3. Post 1 short/day for 7 days
4. Run micro ad tests (₩10k/day)
5. Double down on best creative + audience

## KPI targets (Month 1)
- 30 sales total
- Revenue target: ~₩397,000
- Track: visits, CVR, AOV, CPA, profit

## Budget cap (₩500,000)
- Tooling/domain: ₩120,000
- Product polish: ₩80,000
- Ad tests: ₩200,000
- Reserve: ₩100,000
